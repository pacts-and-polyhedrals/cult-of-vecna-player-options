## What is Cult of Vecna — Player Options?

A homebrew content module built around the forbidden arcane tradition of **Orthogenesis** — the doctrine that nature is not sacred, merely unfinished, and that a sufficiently disciplined wizard can correct it.

At its centre is **Diane Clearwater**: a lich, a scholar, a devoted of Vecna, and a woman who loved a druid named Terrance and returned from death changed enough to make him wonder whether love should survive the answer.

The module includes three full subclasses, 44 spells, a complete bestiary of orthogenic undead and elemental hybrids, and the full text of *The Eight Doors of Knowing* — Diane's poem collection, which looks like grief and functions like a manifesto.

## ✨ Contents

### Subclasses & Features

- 🧙 **School of Orthogenesis** (Wizard) — The forbidden ninth school. Rewrites nature at the biological level. Every feature is a direct philosophical counter to druidic magic.
- ⛪ **Cleric Domain: Whispered Agony** — A Vecna-aligned domain built around pain as revelation and silence as power.
- 🛡 **Paladin Oath: Forbidden Secrets** — An oath sworn not to a god but to the weight of knowing. Break it and Vecna notices.
- 21 class features across all three subclasses

### Spells

- 44 spells across all three subclass lists
- Exclusive Orthogenesis spells: Root Sight, Instinct Tap, Orthogenic Rootbind, Alien Seed, Command the Green, Corrective Bloom, Ninth Door Germination, Elemental Graft, Perfected Beast, Silence of the Grove, The Forest Forgets, Corpse Orchard, Anti-Druidic Zone, Bind the Elemental Root, Wild Shape Undone, Orthogenic Plague, Ecosystem Override, Rewrite the Pattern, The Ninth Verdure, Perfected Genesis

### Lore & GM Notes

- 📖 **The Eight Doors of Knowing** — The complete poem collection by Diane Clearwater. Eight poems (one per school of magic), preface, and closing fragment. The hidden closing line of each poem is bolded for GM reference.
- 📜 **The Orthogenesis Manuscript** — The decoded doctrine and Investigation/Arcana DC table for player discovery.
- 🔮 **Vecna-Warped Orders** — The three organisations that have adopted Orthogenesis, Whispered Agony, and Forbidden Secrets doctrine.
- 💀 **The Phylactery** — Full mechanics for destroying Diane Clearwater permanently, including the four-step destruction sequence.

## ⚙️ GM Features

- **Orthogenic Mark toggle** — Adds a seedling button to all Actor sheets (GM only). One click applies a named Active Effect.
- **Doctrine reveal hook** — When any player rolls 17+ on an Arcana check, the module automatically whispers the decoded eight-part doctrine to the GM in chat.
- **Auto-install dialog** — On first load, prompts the GM to install all content into named world folders automatically.

## Installation

**Manifest URL** (recommended)

```
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pacts-and-polyhedrals/cult-of-vecna-player-options/main/module.json
```

Paste into Foundry: **Settings → Add-on Modules → Install Module**.
